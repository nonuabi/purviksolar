Security Policy

We take the security of purviksolar.com seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability, we appreciate your help in disclosing it responsibly.

Reporting a Vulnerability

Please report security issues by emailing: purviksolar@gmail.com

Include as much information as possible:

  • URL or endpoint affected
  • Steps to reproduce the vulnerability
  • Expected vs actual behavior
  • Any proof-of-concept code (if available)
  • Potential impact of the vulnerability

Guidelines

When reporting vulnerabilities, please follow these guidelines:

  • Do not exploit the vulnerability beyond what is necessary to demonstrate it
  • Do not access or modify user data without explicit permission
  • Do not perform denial-of-service attacks or any activity that could disrupt our services
  • Do not publicly disclose the vulnerability until we have addressed it
  • Do not violate any laws or breach any agreements

Response

We aim to acknowledge security reports within 72 hours and resolve valid issues as quickly as possible. We will keep you informed of our progress throughout the process.

Thank you for helping keep our users and services safe.

Recognition

With your permission, we would be happy to recognize your responsible disclosure in our security acknowledgments (unless you prefer to remain anonymous).
